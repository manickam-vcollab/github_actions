Overview
==========

**VCollab Suite** is the next generation software solution developed by **Visual Collaboration Technologies Inc.,** to provide a unified way to visualize, share, present, document and collaborate CAD, CAE and CAM solution data. It is developed using new advancements in software technologies to provide a more powerful, modular and portable. VCollab Suite products use CAD, CAE and CAM data in the form of CAx files for providing solutions in visualization, presentation, documentation, sharing and collaboration.
